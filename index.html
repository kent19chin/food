“Ground Up, track one off Fire Woman, is a song about building something real out of a dream. The something real that so many of us are fighting for right now is an end to anti-Black racism and white supremacy. The dream that so many of us are longing for is a world where the rights of every human are respected and where every single one of us has access to good water, food, shelter, health care, education and working conditions. We can do it in our lifetime.”

Naila Keleta-Mae
